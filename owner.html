Free Learning Resources

How to Learn Gelatin Art for Free

Whether you want to make your own or just understand the craft behind every cup, these free resources are where the best tutorials live. All of these are completely free to watch and learn from.

YouTube
3D Gelatin Flower Tutorials
Search "3D gelatin art flowers" on YouTube. Dozens of free step by step videos, many in Spanish too. The best visual way to learn the syringe injection technique.
youtube.com → search "3D gelatin art flowers"
YouTube en Español
Gelatinas Artísticas Tutorial
Search "gelatinas artísticas tutorial" for Spanish language tutorials. Many talented creators share complete beginner to advanced techniques at no cost.
youtube.com → "gelatinas artísticas tutorial"
TikTok
Short Form Technique Videos
TikTok is packed with quick gelatin art clips. Watch the syringe technique, see mosaic cup assembly, and get inspired by creators who share their entire process for free.
tiktok.com → search "gelatin art tutorial"
Pinterest
Design Inspiration & Visual Ideas
Pinterest is the best place to find color combination ideas, theme inspiration, and new designs to try. Save your favorite looks as a personal mood board for future orders.
pinterest.com → search "gelatin art"
Facebook Groups
Gelatin Art Community Groups
Search "gelatin art" in Facebook Groups to find communities of makers sharing tips, troubleshooting, and free tutorials. Very welcoming to beginners and full of helpful advice.
facebook.com/groups → search "gelatin art"
Instagram
Follow #GelatinArt
The hashtag #gelatinart and #gelatinasartisticas on Instagram are full of creators who post their work. Many reply to comments and share tips. Great for seeing what sells and what inspires people.
instagram.com → #gelatinart
Marketing & Promotion

Where and How to Promote

You asked the right question. Instagram is absolutely the best starting point and then expanding to Facebook is how you reach your Escondido community fast. Here is exactly how to do it.

📱 Yes, Instagram Is the Right Place to Start

Instagram is the single best platform for a gelatin art business because the product is visually stunning and Instagram is built for beautiful images and short videos. When someone watches a slow pour video or sees a flower being injected petal by petal, they stop scrolling. That is how you build an audience and get orders.

Start your Instagram page at @thefrogandthepanda. Post product videos, share Mia and Logan's story, and use the Reels feature to reach new people in Escondido who don't follow you yet. Then take that exact same content and share it everywhere below.
